**[FoodOnMood](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.zeiq.foodonmood&hl=en_IN&gl=US)** is a food delivery app that allows users to order food from various restaurants and cafes in their area. The app provides menus, prices, and reviews of the restaurants, and allows users to track their orders and receive notifications about delivery times.

**Food on Mood** also offers users the option to provide feedback and ratings on their food and delivery experience, helping other users make informed decisions when ordering from the same restaurants.

Additionally, the app occasionally offers exclusive discounts and deals to its users, making it a cost-effective way to order food online.
