JSL aka Jaspal Singh, is an Indian music director and singer from New Delhi, India. He started his professional journey with his popular cover version of "Kolaveri Di" titled "Kolaveri Punjabified", also he is the guy behind the most rated female award winning track "PARANDA" with KAUR B featuring himself, "PARANDA was in my head for almost n year, i played it to KAUR B, it was more like a gift to her, as i was sure shot it would become a MEGA HIT." he adds. As well as producing hit tracks for, 'Diljit Dosanjh "Fan Bhagat Singh Da" "Miss Lonely" "ELTT - The Judai Song" , JSL became a household name after his MEGA Collabs with artists like Jazzy B, Miss Pooja, Ninja, Ammy Virk, Ranjit Bawa, Jassi Gill, A-Kay etc. "My production style has been described as western, club oriented, catchy, folk and melodic." He is mostly popular for the fusion of Folk South Asian songs with his own taste of Western Music. He is also the Music Director of Punjabi Movies like Bikkar Bai Sentimental & Mukhtiar Chadha.
