This is our E-Commerce Bakery Website which is like an online community where one can find the best bakery supplies, recipes, and tips. Whether you are a buyer looking for baking supplies, a seller seeking to sell your baking products, or simply a baking enthusiast wishing to learn more about the art of baking, KRAVINGS is the destination you have been seeking!

Bakerykart is a unique platform where one can find everything needed to make delicious baked masterpieces from baking ingredients to cake decorating supplies. It brings expertise and equipment at the click of a button to home bakers and baking connoisseurs including professional bakers. Right from bakery ingredients to new innovations in baking technology, Bakerykart provides bakers with inspiration and information all on one platform.

An enterprise of Quidpro Ventures Pvt Lmt, Bakerykart is the first of its kinds in india . It is india's first marketplace dedicated to the bakery industry. It aims to connect all the stakeholders of the bakery industry including sellers across categories like baking equipment, bakery ingredients and baking tools; buyers including home bakers, professional chefs and commercial establishments as well as baking experts like renowned bakers and skilled home bakers.
