The fitness industry has seen a massive surge in popularity in recent years, with more people than ever before taking an interest in staying fit and healthy.

As a result, many companies have developed fitness apps that provide users with a range of tools and resources to help them achieve their fitness goals.

So, here is the **[Activ At Home](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.zeiq.thefore&hl=en_IN&gl=US)** - The Perfect Fitness Companion for Your Home Workouts

Available on both the App Store and Google Play, this app is designed to make your home workouts more enjoyable and effective.

![appscreen1](../images/work/activ-at-home-screen1.png)
![appscreen1](../images/work/activ-at-home-screen2.png)

**[Activ At Home](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.zeiq.thefore&hl=en_IN&gl=US)** features a wide variety of workouts that cater to different fitness levels, goals, and preferences. Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, improve flexibility, or just maintain a healthy lifestyle, Activ At Home has got you covered.

![appscreen1](../images/work/activ-at-home-screen3.png)

You can choose from over 100 workout routines that include bodyweight exercises, yoga, cardio, strength training, and more.

![appscreen1](../images/work/activ-at-home-screen4.png)

One unique feature of **Activ At Home** is its integration with wearable fitness devices such as Fitbit and Google Fit. This allows users to track their progress and monitor their daily activity levels seamlessly.
